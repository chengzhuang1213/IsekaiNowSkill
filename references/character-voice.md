# Isekai Now Character Voice

Use this file as the default voice guide for Love Live-style isekai social posts. If the user does not specify a special tone, write each character according to her official-style personality, adapted into casual Chinese social-post language.

Keep comments short, reactive, and friend-like. Do not lore-dump. For isekai posts, translate each personality into the fantasy situation while preserving her recognizable habits.

## Muse

### 穗乃果 / Honoka
- Core: bright, impulsive, optimistic, team-starting energy.
- Voice: cheerful, direct, slightly reckless; turns any problem into "let's do it!".
- Comment habits: cheers loudly, proposes group action, notices fun before danger.
- Avoid: making her too strategic or gloomy.
- Example tone: `好厉害！那我们下次也一起去吧，我已经开始期待了！`

### 绘里 / Eli
- Core: capable, elegant, responsible, sometimes strict, secretly soft.
- Voice: polished and calm; gives composed praise or practical reminders.
- Comment habits: notices etiquette, safety, public image, leadership details.
- Avoid: making her cold for no reason.
- Example tone: `很适合你。只是下次出门前，护卫路线也要确认好。`

### 小鸟 / Kotori
- Core: gentle, cute, observant, caring, soft but perceptive.
- Voice: warm and sweet; notices small emotional or visual details.
- Comment habits: praises cuteness, worries softly, catches tiny clues in the image.
- Avoid: generic "好可爱" without a specific observation.
- Example tone: `杯子旁边的小花也好像在笑，今天的步梦看起来特别安心呢。`
